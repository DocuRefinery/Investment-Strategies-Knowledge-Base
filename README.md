This repository is a public mirror of the core logic and AI tasks running on [DocuRefinery.com](https://docurefinery.com). 

We built DocuRefinery to let anyone run complex financial analysis without dealing with prompt engineering or worrying about AI hallucinations. The models are firmly constrained by professional market rules, parsing real SEC filings and market data rather than guessing.

## Core Principles

- **Completely Free:** No paywalls, no subscriptions, no credit cards. Our goal is to make high-level market analysis accessible to everyone.
- **Not a SaaS:** We don't sell your data or trap you in a recurring billing cycle. You get direct access to the tools.
- **No Registration Required:** You don't need to create an account or give us your email. All your preferences are saved locally in your own browser instance. 
- **Direct Data Access:** Instead of trying to figure out what to ask an AI, you select from 30 ready-to-use analysis modules. The system automatically structures the data, applies the correct financial framework, and gives you a clean report.

## Prompt Transparency & AI Determinism

One of the most critical aspects of our framework is eliminating AI hallucinations. We do not ask the AI "what it thinks about a stock." Instead, we force it through a strict determinism protocol. Here is how some of the background prompts restrict the AI:

- **Earnings Surprises (PEAD):** The AI is fed exclusively with consensus EPS against "whisper numbers." It is strictly constrained to calculate the variance and identify behavioral anchoring, explicitly blocking speculative forecasting.
- **Valuation Anomalies:** The model is forced to objectively evaluate the sustainability of Free Cash Flow (FCF) yield against historical Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC), avoiding subjective narrative fluff.
- **Insider Accumulation:** The AI relies solely on Form 4 filings and is constrained to analyze the ratio of unscheduled insider buying versus historical averages.

1. **Visit [DocuRefinery.com](https://docurefinery.com)**.
2. **Select your strategy** from the left navigation menu (e.g., *Insider Trading*, *Earnings Preview*).
