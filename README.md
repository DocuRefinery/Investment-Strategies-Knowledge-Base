Global financial intelligence framework featuring 30+ AI-driven strategies in 16 languages. A deterministic, multi-model analysis mirror for 5,000+ symbols.

This repository is a public mirror of the core logic and AI tasks running on [DocuRefinery.com](https://docurefinery.com). 

We built DocuRefinery to let anyone run complex financial analysis without dealing with prompt engineering or worrying about AI hallucinations. The models are firmly constrained by professional market rules, parsing real SEC filings and market data rather than guessing.

## Core Principles

- **Completely Free:** No paywalls, no subscriptions, no credit cards. Our goal is to make high-level market analysis accessible to everyone.
- **Not a SaaS:** We don't sell your data or trap you in a recurring billing cycle. You get direct access to the tools.
- **No Registration Required:** You don't need to create an account or give us your email. All your preferences are saved locally in your own browser instance. 
- **Direct Data Access:** Instead of trying to figure out what to ask an AI, you select from 30 ready-to-use analysis modules. The system automatically structures the data, applies the correct financial framework, and gives you a clean report.

## About the Knowledge Base

This repository tracks the documentation and operational logic for all 30 strategies across 16 different languages. It provides full transparency into how DocuRefinery evaluates things like:

- Earnings Surprises and PEAD (Post-Earnings-Announcement Drift)
- Valuation anomalies
- Insider trading patterns
- Sector rotations

You can access the live platform and test these strategies against thousands of equities directly at [DocuRefinery.com](https://docurefinery.com).
